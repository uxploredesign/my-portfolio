Projects



Impact

My role in this Design is the End-to-End Designer. Impact is a conceptual design I created to solve the problem that thousands of adults have with wanting to lead more eco-friendly lives. I started off with identifying the problem being that people just don’t know where to start nor who to trust, and began researching, ideation, wire framing, usability testing, iteration, and Prototyping.

Swell

My role in this design was the End-to-End Designer. SWELL is a conceptual design I created as a final project for Career Foundry to solve the problem that watersports users have of not being able to spend more time enjoying their watersport activities. I began with identifying the problem, then conducting user surveys and remote moderated user interviews, then moved On to ideation of possible solutions, wire framing, usability testing, iteration, and prototyping.
